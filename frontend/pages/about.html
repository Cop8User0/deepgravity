DEEPGRAVITY

Anonymous & Secure Social Platform

About the Platform

DEEPGRAVITY is built for those who want to share their thoughts, ideas, or photos completely anonymously. The platform does not store or display the identity of the post author in public spaces.

Our mission is to create a safe and free environment for conversations and sharing ideas. All private messages are end-to-end encrypted, ensuring only you and the recipient can read them.

We believe in privacy as a fundamental right. No tracking, no logs, no data collection beyond what is necessary for the platform to function.

Key Features

  • 📝
    Anonymous Posts: Share texts and photos without revealing your identity. Every post appears with an anonymous badge.
  • 💬
    Group Chats: Create rooms and chat with people in real-time using WebSocket technology.
  • 🔒
    Private Messages: Secure, encrypted conversations with friends. AES-256 encryption protects your messages.
  • 👥
    Friends System: Add friends, track their online status, and build your trusted network.
  • 🎨
    Dark Glassmorphism Design: A stunning dark theme with frosted glass effects, purple-cyan gradients, and smooth animations.
  • ⚡
    Real-Time Updates: Chat messages, friend requests, and notifications arrive instantly via WebSocket.

Technology Stack

Frontend HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JS (ES6+)
Backend Python (Flask + Flask-SocketIO)
Database SQLite / PostgreSQL
Real-Time WebSocket (Socket.IO)
Auth JWT + bcrypt
Encryption AES-256 (E2E)
